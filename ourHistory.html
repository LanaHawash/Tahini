A Legacy of Quality and Tradition:
The Story of Abdallah Wasef and Har Bracha Tahini

The story of Abdallah Wasef and the Har Bracha Tahini brand traces back to the late 20th century in a small town where Abdallah Wasef began a humble but ambitious journey. With little more than a dedication to quality and a keen eye for high-grade ingredients, he entered the world of sesame trade in 1997. Abdallah knew that the foundation of every exceptional tahini product is only as good as its source, and for him, that meant focusing on the finest sesame seeds in the world. Through research, he identified the rich Ethiopian soil and climate as ideal conditions for cultivating sesame seeds with unique flavor profiles, a high oil content, and nutritional quality.

Abdallah began trading Ethiopian sesame seeds, investing long hours into building relationships with farmers, understanding the intricacies of cultivation, and ensuring his seeds were sourced responsibly and sustainably. He quickly gained a reputation in his community and beyond as a supplier of top-quality sesame seeds. By maintaining a deep-rooted commitment to the purest ingredients, Abdallah Wasef laid the groundwork for what would soon become a family legacy.

The Birth of Abdallah Wasef and Sons: A Family Dream

As the years passed, Abdallah’s sons joined the family business, learning the trade from the ground up. With a shared vision and values, they established "Abdallah Wasef and Sons" together. This family-centered business was founded on principles of respect, integrity, and dedication to quality, all of which were key lessons Abdallah passed down to his children. Abdallah's sons added fresh ideas, an entrepreneurial spirit, and boundless energy to the business, taking their father’s legacy to new heights. Together, they began to dream bigger, seeking not only to trade sesame but to create products that would highlight the seeds' quality and potential.

The family soon recognized an opportunity: tahini. They knew that if made traditionally, tahini could preserve the authentic, creamy flavor of sesame while offering a healthy, natural product loved by people worldwide. Abdallah Wasef and his sons set out to learn the traditional methods for tahini production, consulting with local artisans and even venturing to regions where tahini-making was an age-old practice. They became experts in every step of the tahini-making process, from roasting to grinding, ensuring that each batch of tahini would retain the pure taste of sesame seeds.

A New Chapter with Har Bracha Tahini: A Vision Fulfilled

With a clear vision and unrelenting dedication, Abdallah Wasef and his sons partnered with Abdallah’s cousins, who shared their commitment to creating premium tahini. In the early 2000s, they founded Har Bracha Tahini. The name "Har Bracha" translates to "Mount of Blessing," symbolizing the blessings of nature and family unity upon which the business was built. By this point, the Wasef family had firmly established a reputation for quality, and Har Bracha Tahini was set to carry on that legacy.

The production process at Har Bracha Tahini remains deeply rooted in tradition. Abdallah and his team continue to use the stone-grinding method, a technique passed down through generations, which ensures a smooth, rich, and full-bodied tahini. Each batch undergoes a meticulous process, from carefully selecting and roasting the seeds to grinding them slowly for a smooth, consistent texture that sets Har Bracha Tahini apart from the competition. This attention to detail and respect for tradition has resulted in a product known for its high quality and rich flavor profile.

Recognition and Growth: From Local Heritage to Global Impact

The dedication to quality at Har Bracha Tahini did not go unnoticed. Soon after its establishment, Har Bracha Tahini gained attention from local communities, leading to a loyal customer base that appreciated the authentic, robust flavors and smooth texture. But the impact didn’t stop there. With word of mouth and positive reviews, Har Bracha Tahini began attracting international interest. Distributors from across the globe reached out, eager to bring this unique product to new markets. As the brand grew, Abdallah and his family remained true to their mission, focusing on quality over quantity, which earned them respect and recognition in international food communities.

Today, Har Bracha Tahini exports to numerous countries worldwide, supplying households, restaurants, and gourmet stores with its signature tahini. The brand has become a symbol of authenticity, quality, and the timeless value of family traditions. Every jar of Har Bracha Tahini is a testament to the Wasef family’s journey, carrying with it the story of Abdallah's humble beginnings and the unity of a family committed to excellence. Their products are a celebration of heritage, bringing the blessings of high-quality tahini to customers around the world.
